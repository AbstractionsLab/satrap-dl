# CTI SKB content: organizational CTI

SATRAP SHALL provide a mechanism to retrieve information from the CyFORT CTI repository and integrate it into the CTI SKB.

### Rationale
To integrate CTI knowledge relevant for the organization. Organizational CTI refers here to CTI internally produced or
shared by other organizations in a CTI sharing network.
